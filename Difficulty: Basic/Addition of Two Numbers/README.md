# Sum of Two Numbers

## Problem Statement

Given two numbers `a` and `b`. Your task is to return the **sum** of `a` and `b`.

---

## Examples

### Example 1  
**Input:**  
`a = 1`, `b = 2`  
**Output:**  
`3`  
**Explanation:**  
Addition of 1 and 2 is 3.

---

### Example 2  
**Input:**  
`a = 10`, `b = 20`  
**Output:**  
`30`  
**Explanation:**  
Addition of 10 and 20 is 30.

---

## Constraints

- `1 ≤ a, b ≤ 10^18`
