# Even Factor Sum

## Problem Statement
Given an integer **N**, find the **sum of all even factors** of N.

---

## Examples

**Input:**

N = 30

**Output:**

48

**Explanation:**  
Even divisors of 30 are 2, 6, 10, and 30 → sum = 48.

---

**Input:**

N = 18

**Output:**

26

**Explanation:**  
Even divisors of 18 are 2, 6, and 18 → sum = 26.

---

## Constraints

1 ≤ N ≤ 10^9


**Expected Time Complexity:** O(√N)  
**Expected Space Complexity:** O(1)
