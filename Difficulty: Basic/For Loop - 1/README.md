You are given a number `n`, and you need to print its **multiplication table** from `n * 1` to `n * 10` in a **single line** using a `for` loop.

---

### Examples

**Input:**  
`n = 5`  
**Output:**  
`5 10 15 20 25 30 35 40 45 50`

---

**Input:**  
`n = 6`  
**Output:**  
`6 12 18 24 30 36 42 48 54 60`

---

### Constraints:
- `1 <= n <= 100`
