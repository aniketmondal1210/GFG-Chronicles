# Factorial of a Number

## Problem Statement
Given a positive integer `N`, calculate its **factorial**.

The factorial of a number `N` is defined as:

N! = 1 × 2 × 3 × ... × N


---

## Example 1
**Input:**

5

**Output:**

120

**Explanation:**

1 × 2 × 3 × 4 × 5 = 120


---

## Constraints

1 ≤ N ≤ 18

**Expected Time Complexity:** O(N)  
**Expected Space Complexity:** O(1)
