# Sum of Digits of a Number

## Problem Statement
You are given an integer `n`.  
Your task is to calculate the **sum of its digits**.

---

## Examples

### Example 1
**Input:**  

n = 1

**Output:**  

1

**Explanation:**  
Sum of digit of `1` is `1`.

---

### Example 2
**Input:**  

n = 99999

**Output:**  

45

**Explanation:**  
`9 + 9 + 9 + 9 + 9 = 45`.

---

## Constraints
- `1 ≤ n ≤ 10^7`  
- **Expected Time Complexity:** `O(log10(n))` (number of digits)  
- **Expected Auxiliary Space:** `O(1)`
