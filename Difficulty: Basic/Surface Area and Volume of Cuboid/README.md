# Cuboid Surface Area and Volume

## Problem
Given the length `l`, width `b`, and height `h` of a cuboid, find:
1. **Total Surface Area (TSA)**
2. **Volume**

---

## Formula
- **Surface Area** = 2 × (l × b + b × h + h × l)  
- **Volume** = l × b × h

---

## Examples

### Example 1
**Input:**

l = 1, b = 2, h = 3

**Output:**

22 6

**Explanation:**  
Surface area = 2 × (2 × 3 + 3 × 1 + 1 × 2) = 22  
Volume = 1 × 2 × 3 = 6

---

### Example 2
**Input:**

l = 2, b = 3, h = 5

**Output:**

62 30

**Explanation:**  
Surface area = 2 × (3 × 5 + 5 × 2 + 2 × 3) = 62  
Volume = 2 × 3 × 5 = 30

---

## Constraints
- 1 ≤ l, b, h ≤ 100
