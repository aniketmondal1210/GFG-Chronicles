# Area of a Circle

## Problem Statement

Given the radius of circle r. The task is to find the area of circle.

## Examples

### Example 1:
**Input:**  
`r = 5`  
**Output:**  
`78.53982`  
**Explanation:**  
Area = π × 5 × 5 = 78.53982

### Example 2:
**Input:**  
`r = 2`  
**Output:**  
`12.56637`  
**Explanation:**  
Area = π × 2 × 2 = 12.56637

---

## Constraints

- `0 <= r <= 10^5`
