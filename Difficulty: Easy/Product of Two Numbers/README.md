# Product of Two Integers

## Problem Statement

You are given two integers `x` and `y`. Your task is to return the **product** of `x` and `y`.

---

## Examples

### Example 1:
**Input:**  
`x = 5`, `y = 2`  
**Output:**  
`10`  
**Explanation:**  
The product of 5 and 2 is `5 × 2 = 10`.

---

### Example 2:
**Input:**  
`x = 100`, `y = 5`  
**Output:**  
`500`  
**Explanation:**  
The product of 100 and 5 is `100 × 5 = 500`.

---

## Constraints

- `-10⁴ ≤ x, y ≤ 10⁴`
